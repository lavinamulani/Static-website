FACTS ABOUT JUPITER
  • With a radius of 43,440.7 miles (69,911 kilometers), Jupiter is 11 times wider than Earth.
  • Jupiter is the fifth planet fromthe Sunin the solar system and the largest of all.
  • Jupiter is so massive that its total mass is twice as much as the mass of all planets in Solar System combined together.
  • Jupiter is the third brightest planet in the night sky after Earth’s moon and Venus.
  • Jupiter has the shortest day of all the planets. One day on Jupiter takes about 10 hours.
